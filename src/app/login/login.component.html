Welcome to Foodie!!

Foodie is specially for knowing about the whereabouts of your favourite food dishes and also about the food items which you dont know. But how? Here is the way..

-Find/capture a photo of the food item.

-Upload the photo by clicking 'Add image' button.

-Lastly, click the 'Upload photo' button.

That's it! Doing this, you get the recipes and locations of where the dish could be found.
